About Us

Providing Quality Healthcare with Compassion

Our Story

Copains Referral Hospital has been serving the community since its establishment in 1985. Over the years, we have grown to become a leading healthcare institution dedicated to providing comprehensive medical services with a focus on patient-centered care.

Mission & Vision

Our mission is to improve the health and well-being of our patients by delivering high-quality, compassionate healthcare services. We envision a healthier community where every individual has access to excellent medical care when they need it.

Our Values

  • Compassion - We treat every patient with kindness and empathy.
  • Integrity - We uphold the highest ethical standards in all our actions.
  • Excellence - We strive for excellence in patient care and clinical outcomes.
  • Patient-Centered - We prioritize the needs and preferences of our patients.
